HOW DO WE DO IT

The question that's on your mind is, how do we make our clothing? It's quite simple really, first we crack a few Yogis to put its insides onto our mixture, we then stir in order to create this lovely gold pattern. We then use this mixture to dye our clothes, which is how my company gets its signature styling.
